FAQ

How do I upload and convert an image to a 3D model?
On the Home page, click the upload area and select a JPEG/PNG/WebP image. The system processes it and shows a 3D preview on the right.
What formats are supported?
Images: JPEG, PNG, WebP. Generated 3D models are exported as GLB, usable directly in compatible viewers.
How do I download the generated 3D model?
Click the "Download GLB" button in the preview area or on the gallery card to download the model file.
What browsers are supported?
Modern browsers (Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox) are supported. If the 3D preview behaves unexpectedly, please update your browser.
What if loading is slow or the model appears blank?
Check your network and file integrity. The page includes placeholders and error hints; if issues persist, refresh the page or re-upload.
How are gallery categories (Object/Car/Helmet/Role) determined?
Categories are mapped by filename or specific rules. If you request changes, we will update both the mapping and filter chips.

Check whether your prompt is clear and specific, confirm the uploaded image is sharp and in a supported format, and ensure a stable network connection. If problems persist, contact support or try generating again.
